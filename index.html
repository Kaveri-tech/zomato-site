Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Zomato?

▼

Zomato is a global restaurant discovery and food delivery platform. Users can explore restaurants, view menus, read reviews, and order food online.

2. How can I order food through Zomato?

▼

You can easily order food by searching for your desired restaurant or dish, adding items to the cart, and completing the order by providing delivery details and payment.

3. Does Zomato offer contactless delivery?

▼

Yes, Zomato offers the option for contactless delivery where the delivery partner will leave your food at your door, ensuring safe and secure delivery.

4. Can I add my restaurant to Zomato?

▼

Yes, restaurant owners can add their restaurant to Zomato by clicking on the "Add Restaurant" option in the menu, filling in the required details, and following the registration process.

5. How do I contact customer support?

▼

You can contact Zomato's customer support through the "Help" section in the app or website, where you can submit your queries and get assistance from our support team.
